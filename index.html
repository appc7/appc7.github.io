About Me

I am a graduate student pursuing master degree in computer science, with broad interests in computer technologies. Now I am working in the laboratory of Dr. Ali — DSSL (Distributed Systems & Storage Laboratory). Especially, my research interests lie in improving the performance of memory efficiency in memory processing frameworks and large scale in-­memory data analytic platforms, like spark. Try to find the optimal level of parallelism and repartition data as needed to ensure efficient task granularity, avoid data skew and reduce shuffle traffic. In addition, Dr. Ali and Dr. Na are my advisors. Dr. Na focuses on Software Engineering and Programming Languages, therefore I am also interesed in code clone detection and issues of programming languages, like security vulnerabilities.
